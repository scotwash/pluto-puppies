The Jackalope

Description

Fun Facts

The Jackalope is a mythical creature described as a Jackrabbit with Antelope horns. They originated as a cryptid from Wyoming in the 1930s when two hunter brothers decided to taxidermy and graft antlers onto a Jackrabbit which they sold to people. However there were sightings of them as far back as the 1820s and art depictions of them dated as far back as 1575 in Europe, Asia, and Africa.

The Jackalope is so popular in Wyoming that the Wyoming Legislature has considered making bills to pass the Jackalope as the states offical mythological creature. The first Jackalope head made by the brothers was sold for 10 dollars and then stolen in 1977 and hasn't been found since. Artist renditions of them have been seen in shows and games such as Scooby Doo, Gravity Falls, Red Dead, and My Singing Monsters.

They are seen by Folklorists as Tall Tales creatures, also known as Fearsome critters. They most commonly are described in tall tales along side other similiar beasts like hodags, fur trout, and giant snakes.

The Jackalope is believed by some to be spotting a Jackrabbit with the Shope papilloma virus, Which causes horn like growths to sprout from animal's head. The Jackalope is also recorded as an offical SCP in the SCP database as SCP-7134.
