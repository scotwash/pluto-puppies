Cryptids Around North America

North America is covered head to toe in creatures called "cryptids". Cryptids are most commonly defined as creatures that are believed to exist but who have no scientific evidence behind them. These include Mothman, the Chupacabra, and more! Our group of researchers have taken the time to research our favorite ones roaming North America and share our knowledge with you!
